Everything you need to plan your SoCal adventures without getting sunburned, stranded, or surprised by a parking situation nobody warned you about.

## Weather & Conditions

<div class="nct-resource-grid">
  <div class="nct-resource-card">
    <h3><a href="https://forecast.weather.gov/" target="_blank">NWS Point Forecast</a></h3>
    <p>Real forecast data. Especially critical for desert trips where temperature differences between day and night can exceed 50°F.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="nct-resource-card">
    <h3><a href="https://www.purpleair.com/map" target="_blank">PurpleAir Map</a></h3>
    <p>Real-time air quality. Essential during fire season, which in SoCal can mean September through December.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="nct-resource-card">

</div>
